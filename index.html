Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the recovery process take?
Our typical recovery period is 1-3 months, depending on the complexity of the case. Some straightforward cases might be resolved sooner, while more complex international cases may take longer.
What happens if I don't pay the fee?
The fee is only due once we successfully recover your funds. If you do not pay the recovery fee, the recovered funds will remain in escrow and recovery efforts will be halted. The fee covers our operational costs and legal expenses.
What types of cases do you handle?
We specialize in recovering funds from various types of financial fraud, including: Forex scams, cryptocurrency fraud, binary options schemes, investment frauds, romance scams, phishing attacks, and unauthorized crypto transactions.
What is your success rate?
We maintain a 93% success rate across all our cases, with over $50 million recovered for our clients. Our high success rate is due to our experienced team of legal experts and advanced tracking technologies.
How do I start the recovery process?
Simply login to your account using one of our secure authentication methods and provide your case details. Our team will review your case within 24 hours and begin the recovery process immediately.
What information do I need to provide?
You'll need to provide details about your case including: transaction history, communication with the scammers, any contracts or agreements, and proof of payments. The more information you provide, the better we can assist you.
Do you guarantee recovery?
While we have a high success rate, we cannot guarantee recovery in every case. However, we only charge our fee upon successful recovery of your funds, so there's no risk in trying our services.
Is my information secure?
Yes, we use bank-level encryption and security measures to protect all client information. Your data is stored on secure servers and is only accessible to authorized personnel working on your case.
Can you help with international cases?
Yes, we handle cases globally and have successfully recovered funds from scammers in multiple countries. Our team has experience dealing with international law enforcement and financial institutions.
